Case Studies

Short examples that show how I work.

One-week metadata backfill for new MAM rollout (Sports)

Scope

Ahead of a new MAM system rollout, a large volume of sports metadata needed to be entered within a one-week deadline, with high risk of duplication and missed items.

What I did

  • As the most senior team member on a 3-person group, I organised a “war-room” setup for the week, booked a dedicated room, and introduced a clear allocation system (whiteboard tracking by sport/category). We logged progress in real time to avoid duplicates and keep throughput high.

Result

We completed the required metadata entry within the deadline and helped ensure the MAM system was ready for rollout and training.

Prevented incorrect language delivery (TX deadline)

Scope

A Portuguese-language asset was routed to me and I identified it was Brazilian Portuguese instead of the required European Portuguese, with TX approaching and no correct dub immediately available for legacy content.

What I did

  • I stopped the asset from progressing, verified requirements, and launched a targeted search across internal libraries, regional contacts, and relevant teams/vendors to locate the correct EU Portuguese dub. I kept stakeholders updated and tracked options until a confirmed version was secured.

Result

The correct European Portuguese dub was delivered in time for TX, preventing an on-air language error and ensuring compliance with the transmission requirement.
